Authly Authenticator — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Authly Authenticator (“the App”) is a local, offline authenticator and password utility for macOS. Your privacy is simple because the App is built to keep everything on your device.

Data we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

Your authenticator secrets and any saved passwords are stored only on your Mac, in the system Keychain. They never leave your device and are never sent to us or any third party. The App makes no network connections for this data.

Camera

If you choose to scan a QR code, the App uses your camera solely to read the code on-device. Camera images are processed locally and are never stored or transmitted.

Files you import or export

When you import (QR images, CSV) or export your data, the App only reads or writes the specific files you select. It does not access any other files.

Children’s privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
