Authly Authenticator

Fast, private 2FA codes right from your Mac menu bar.

What it does

Authly keeps your two-factor (TOTP) codes and passwords on your Mac and one click away in the menu bar. Everything stays on your device — no account, no cloud, no tracking. Codes are generated offline (RFC 6238).

Support / FAQ

How do I add an account? Click the Authly menu bar icon, then use the “+” button, the camera (scan a QR code), or Import (a QR image or a CSV exported from Apple Passwords, Chrome, or 1Password). You can also paste an otpauth:// URI.

Where is my data stored? Only on your Mac, in the system Keychain. It is never uploaded anywhere.

How do I move my codes to another device? Use Export to save your accounts as standard otpauth QR images, then scan them in any authenticator app.
